# Remote Workflow Pack v1

## Purpose

This pack is designed for cold-reader testing of the Remote Workflow Opportunity Brief artifact.

## Materials to show

- brief pack index
- one promoted brief
- one watchlist brief

Suggested examples:
- promoted: `case_01_gc_8.html`
- watchlist: `case_03_gc_9.html`

## What to evaluate

- Does the reader understand what this artifact is for?
- Can they tell why one case is Promoted and another is Watchlist?
- Do they know what to do next from each brief?
- What feels unclear, too technical, or unconvincing?
